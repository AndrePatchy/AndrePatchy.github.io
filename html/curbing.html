Curbing Employee Attrition

Context

The HR team collected data from employees and we've been asked to predict the attrition of an employee, i.e. the probability of an employee with certain characteristics stay or quit his/her current job in the company. We've must build a model to use by this company's HR team warning them about employees that potentially will quit.
