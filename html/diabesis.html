Solution

Different CNN architectures were tested, including dropout, local histogram equalization, and data augmentation strategies. The VGG-16 model was also employed for comparison. All models demonstrated high accuracy in identifying diabetic retinopathy, with the VGG-16 model showing exceptional performance (AUC score: 0.97).
